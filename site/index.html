Math & Reading App for Kids Ages 5–10

Daily math and reading missions for kids aged 5-10. Learn, play, repeat!

What makes Geni special

Parents can see the rhythm of the app before downloading: quick wins, guided reading, rewards, privacy, and a setup built for family life.

🔢Math flow

Math Missions

Short exercises feel fast and game-like, with progress, instant feedback, and the next challenge ready right away.

📖Reading flow

Reading Adventures

Words stay large and focused, with read-to-me, read-by-myself, and read-aloud modes that guide attention line by line.

🏆Reward loop

Rewards & Badges

Coins, XP, streaks, and badge unlocks turn steady practice into momentum that kids want to keep going.

🔒Parent side

Parent Controls

A protected parent area keeps setup simple: enter the PIN, switch profiles, review progress, and change settings.

📱Private by design

Offline & Private

The app works without internet and keeps data on the device, so the experience stays focused, fast, and ad-free.

👶Family setup

Multiple Profiles

Siblings can share one app while keeping their own avatars, level progress, and age-appropriate difficulty.

🌍Language support

Supported Languages

English, Norwegian, Spanish, and Portuguese are built into the app, so families can choose the language that fits them best.
